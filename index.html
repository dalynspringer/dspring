Photography in my life

Photography has been a big part of my life for a while. In high school I joined the yearbook staff, and I loved getting to walk around with a camera and capture moments from games, assemblies, and everyday school life. It was fun knowing my photos would end up in the yearbook and mean something to people later. When I went to college, I brought an old camera I found to capture the moments of college. Using that camera has made me slow down, capture moments, and create memories that will be visable for generations.

Top 3 Photography Tips

  1. Pay attention to light
    • Use sunrise and sunset for softer, warmer color.
    • Look for side light to show texture.
  2. Keep the frame simple
    • Move closer instead of relying on zoom.
    • Avoid cluttered backgrounds when possible.
  3. Add a story element
    • Include foreground, midground, and background.
    • Think about what the viewer should feel.
